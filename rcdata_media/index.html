A message from the site creator

Thank you for taking the time to visit rc-stats. I created this site in my free time to help contribute back to the sport I enjoy so much. I have been working hard to incorporate feedback throughout the development process and I want to continue to refine and improve the site. If you have a suggestion on how the site could be easier to use, or a feature that would be useful, I will do my best to help.

I am working to make sure this site it is supported across the popular browsers and devices, but if you spot a bug or you are unable to use certain site functionality on a given device, please let me know.

I look forward to racing with you, thanks!

About rc-stats.com

rc-stats.com is dedicated to the rc racing community. This is a site to store race results so that they can be easily organized and reviewed. Whether your goal is improving your lap times or just taking your bench racing to the next level, rc-stats will have the tools for you.

This site was designed to be extensible, so you if you want your track or event included please contact an admin. Created by racers for racers.

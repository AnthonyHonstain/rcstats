Meet the Team

All off the work to design, build, and maintain the rc-stats site comes from volunteers. This is not just a one man effort, but a collective effort by enthusiasts throughout the racing community. We are passionate about RC racing and the growth of the sport, this site is the embodiment of that vision. If you see one of the following individuals please thank them because without their effort and support this would not be possible.

The tracks that make the effort to upload results and engage the racing community are also deserving of your support and patronage.

About rc-stats.com

rc-stats.com is dedicated to the rc racing community. This is a site to store race results so that they can be easily organized and reviewed. Whether your goals are doing a statistical analysis of race or just taking your bench racing to the next level, rc-stats will have the tools for you.

This site was designed to be extensible, so you if you want your track or event included please contact an admin. Created by a club racer in Pacific Northwest, I hope you can find the site useful. Created by racers for racers.

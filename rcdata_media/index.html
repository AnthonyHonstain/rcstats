NEW - Ranking

A Ranking system by track and class has been added! You can see the current leader board and look at your progress over time (compared to similarly ranked racers). This is a FIRST in remote control car racing, now any track can have a free ranking system for club races. As more results are compiled we will have the ability to rank and compare across entire regions.

Overview

Ranking systems are used in many other competitive sports, like chess and online games. I have used the race results that I collected (2011-current) in combination with an open source implementation based on trueskill to create an initial ranking for Stock Buggy racers. This is not the finished implementation, this is a trial run.

I highly recommend reading - http://www.moserware.com/2010/03/computing-your-skill.html

·         The ranking system is only an estimate of your skill.  It is possible that you are changing faster than the algorithm can account for; it may take several events for the algorithm to get a better approximation of your skill.

·         Your ranking should not be decrease if you lose to higher ranked racers.

·         If you were destroying the field in 2011, but you have only driven a bit in 2012, your rank might not be a very good estimate of your skill level.

·         I am still going through the algorithm and the implementation for calculating the ranking may change significantly.

·         I am unsure of what the implications are of having multiple tracks that do not co-mingle often. It is possible that I may give additional weight to more important events.

What is the difference between this and counting win/losses etc? This system starts with an estimate of your ability relative to your competitors and a confidence value for the estimate. As new race results are processed, the estimate in your ability  will change and the system can become more confident in its estimation.

 

Possible Features.

·         Implement a page to have real time rank upgrades with a graph to track progress.

·         Implement  rank decay to keep the ranks moving.

·         Keep track ranking separate.

·         Reset scores several times a year.

 

Goals

My goals for the ranking system are simple: for the system to be a positive influence on the racing community.  I find this kind of information very valuable in evaluating if changes in my kit or extra practice are having an impact.

 

Transparency

Source code can be viewed here: https://github.com/iteratorrc/trueskill If you would like to run the calculations yourself, please contact me and I will get you a dataset. I welcome any feedback or recommendations.

Assumptions and Details

·         The initial results are restricted to stock buggy main events. No qualifiers. I can do other classes, but stock buggy has the most consistent turn out.

·         I have grouped B,C,D, … main events into a single race with the A-main to get a single event to calculate ranking off of.

·         I am only displaying competitors with 10 or more race results.

·         I am only displaying the top half of the ranking (if you are not in here, you might be in the bottom half or you may not have 10 races in the database yet).
